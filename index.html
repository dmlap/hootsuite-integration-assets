JW Player Connatix Media Library App

Integrate your JW Player Connatix videos with Hootsuite's social media management platform

Overview Features Setup Usage

Overview

The JW Player Connatix Media Library App lets you access your JW Player Connatix videos directly within Hootsuite. Add videos to your social media posts without switching between platforms.

Features

  • Search: Find videos by title, description, or tags
  • Browse: Navigate through your video library with pagination
  • Secure: OAuth 2.0 authentication with encrypted credentials

Setup

You'll need your JW Player Connatix API key and Site ID. Find these in your JW Player Connatix Dashboard.

1

Install the App

Go to Hootsuite Apps section and find "JW Player Connatix Media Library"

2

Enter Credentials

Provide your Site ID and API key when prompted

3

Authorize

Click "Authorize" to complete the setup

Usage

Once connected, access your videos when creating posts in Hootsuite:

1

Browse Videos

Click the media attachment icon in your compose window

2

Select Source

Choose "JW Player Connatix" from the media options

3

Add to Post

Click on a video to add it to your post
